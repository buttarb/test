Services

Martha Lake Veterinary Clinic is proud to offer the finest healthcare service to the pets of Lynnwood, WA and the surrounding areas. Our mission is to provide quality care for your furry (and not so furry) friends.  Serivces include advanced surgeries, preventative care, wide range of diagnostics, digital radiography, dental care, internal medicine, flea prevention, and much more.

Meet the Doctors

Dr. Pal acquired Cedar Plaza Vet in June 2012. He graduated from Punjab Agricultural University from State of Punjab, India 2004. Further he achieved Master's (2006) in Veterinary Parasitology from GADVASU, India and PhD (2010) in Animal Sciences and WSU, Pullman WA. Since moving to WA state in 2006 Dr. Pal has made the Pacific Northwest his home and currently lives in Bothell WA with wife Sonia, daughter and son.
